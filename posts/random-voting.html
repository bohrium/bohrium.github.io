QUADRATIC VOTING IN THE SENATE

Aesthetics

encourage deliberation, discussion, trade, compromise. permit but penalize extremism.

Implementation

procedure

Voting with integer weight $n$ on a bill costs ${n \choose 2}$ points. No senator may spend more than $N=100$ points during any term. A bill passes if and only if it receives strictly more than $11/20$ths of the unweighted vote and strictly more than $1/2$ of the weighted vote.
